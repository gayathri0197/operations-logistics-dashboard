# 🚚 Operations & Logistics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end Power BI dashboard built using a logistics and supply chain dataset to analyse operational performance, delivery efficiency, shipment risk, and cost impact.

The dashboard was designed to turn complex operational data into actionable insights that can support better decision-making in logistics and supply chain management.

---

## 🎯 Objective
The goal of this project was to create an interactive dashboard that helps track key logistics KPIs, identify operational bottlenecks, and evaluate factors contributing to shipment delays and supply chain risk.

This project focuses on bridging the gap between raw logistics data and business-focused operational insights.

## 📌 Key Features

**Overview Page**
- **KPI Cards** — Total Shipments (32K), Total Shipping Cost (£14.73M), 
  Delay Rate (82.09%), High Risk Shipment% (53.59%)
- **Shipments & Shipping Cost by Month/Year** — Dual-axis trend chart (2021–2024)
- **Shipments by Risk Category** — Donut chart (High 53.59% / Moderate 24.19% / Low 22.21%)
- **Historical Demand vs Customs Clearance Efficiency** — Monthly comparison
- **Disruption Likelihood Score by Month** — Line trend for risk forecasting
- **Driver Score & Fuel Consumption** — Animated monthly breakdown

**Operational Performance Page**
- **KPI Cards** — Avg Delay Deviation (5.18), Total Delayed Hours (173.16K), 
  OTD% (17.91%), Avg Arrival Delay (2.89)
- **Driver Behaviour vs Delay Deviation** — Scatter analysis
- **Impact of Traffic Congestion on Delays** — Bar chart by congestion level
- **OTD% by Risk Category** — Horizontal bar comparison
- **Shipping Cost by Carrier & Risk Category** — Matrix table + treemap visual

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Over half of all shipments (53.59%) fall into the High Risk category, 
  directly impacting total shipping costs
- On-time delivery sits at only 17.91%, highlighting significant 
  bottlenecks in the delivery cycle
- Higher traffic congestion levels correlate strongly with increased delay deviations
- Premium carriers carry the highest shipping cost share across all risk categories
- Moderate Risk shipments slightly outperform High Risk on OTD% (18.07% vs 18.04%)
